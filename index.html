About

Hi! I am a Year-2 PhD candidate at AIM group of The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. I received my M.S. degree from Institue of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), where I am grateful to be advised by Prof.Wenting Shang, Prof.Di Dong, and Prof.Jie Tian. Before that, I received my double Bachelor's degree in Information Engineering and Finance from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU).

My research interest lies in Generative Modeling and AI for Healthcare, especially multi-modality joint representation learning for medical vision-language Models and Agents.
